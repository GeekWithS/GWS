The Story

Making Tech Accessible For Everyone

GWS is more than just a channel; it's a student movement in Sri Lanka's tech landscape.

Geek With S (GWS) is a technology-focused media platform created by passionate students. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical ICT knowledge and real-world application. We simplify complex concepts—from coding to cybersecurity—ensuring that every student, regardless of their background, can master the digital skills required for the future.
