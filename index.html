About Us

What is GWS?

A short story of who we are and what we're building together.

Geek With S (GWS) is a technology-focused YouTube channel created by passionate students from Sri Lanka. Our goal is to make ICT learning simple, practical and enjoyable for everyone — whether you're a total beginner or an aspiring tech professional. We believe that great education shouldn't be complicated, and that students can lead the way in creating meaningful learning experiences.
